Cavalier King Charles Spaniel Information

Historically the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel was a lap dog and is small for a spaniel, with fully grown adults comparable in size to adolescents of other larger spaniel breeds. Breed standards state that height of a Cavalier should be between 12 to 13 inches (30 to 33 cm) with a proportionate weight between 10 to 18 pounds (4.5 to 8.2 kg).

They come in 4 different colors
  • Blenheim
  • Tricoulor
  • Ruby
  • Black and Tan
